About the Doctor

A cardiologist who listens before he prescribes

Dr. Karimi completed his cardiology training at the Royal College of Physicians, London, and undertook subspecialty fellowship in interventional cardiology at the Cleveland Clinic. He returned to Pakistan to build a practice founded on evidence-based medicine and genuine patient partnership.

His clinical focus spans acute coronary syndromes, structural heart disease, and preventive cardiology. Known for explaining complex diagnoses in plain language — and for being reachable when it matters most.

Services

Comprehensive cardiac care, under one roof

Initial Consultation

How It Works

From first call to clear answers

A straightforward path — no unnecessary waits, no repeat tests you've already had.

1

Book Online or by Phone

Choose your appointment type, preferred date, and whether you're a new or returning patient. Confirmation within one business day.

2

Arrive & Consult

Bring any previous reports and imaging. Dr. Karimi reviews them before you're called in. No rushed ten-minute slots.

3

Diagnostics if Needed

On-site ECG, echo, or blood tests ordered and, where possible, performed the same day. No unnecessary return visits.

4

Treatment Plan & Follow-up

A written summary and clear next steps. Follow-up appointments, medication, or referrals arranged before you leave.
